About The Plant Finder

Whether you're a seasoned gardener or just starting your plant journey, the Plant Finder website makes it easy to discover, learn about, and care for plants that suit your space and lifestyle. Instead of searching through books or scattered sources, you get instant access to detailed plant information — including sunlight needs, watering habits, indoor/outdoor suitability, and more — all in one place. It's a smarter, faster way to choose the perfect plant for your home or garden and keep it thriving.
